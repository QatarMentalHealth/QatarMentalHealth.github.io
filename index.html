The Qatar Mental Health Study is a study conducted by American School of Doha students, which aims to understand the effects of the COVID-19 pandemic on the mental health of adolescents. This website is part of their contribution to the improvemnt of mental wellbeing for adolescents, through providing a mental health diagnosis and multiple therapy resources.
